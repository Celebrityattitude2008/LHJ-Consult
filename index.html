Judith Obiefule

Certified Health & Nutrition Strategist

"Judith Obiefule is the visionary founder of LHJ Consult, a faith-based wellness consultancy dedicated to transforming lives through holistic health practices. With over 15 years of experience in health and nutrition, she has helped hundreds of individuals and families discover the profound connection between physical wellness and spiritual vitality. As a Certified Health and Nutrition Strategist, Judith combines evidence-based nutritional science with faith-centered guidance to create personalized wellness journeys. Her signature "21-Day Fruits2Fitness" challenge has empowered countless participants—affectionately known as "Comrades"—to achieve lasting health transformations. Judith's approach is rooted in the belief that true wellness encompasses body, mind, and spirit. Her warm, compassionate style and unwavering commitment to her clients' success have made her a trusted voice in the Nigerian wellness community."
